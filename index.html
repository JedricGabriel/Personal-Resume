About Me

I am a BSIT graduate from New Era University with a deep-rooted passion for cybersecurity and digital defense. My journey into the world of information security began with a fascination for understanding how systems can be protected against increasingly sophisticated threats.

What drives me is the intellectual challenge of ethical hacking and the continuous pursuit of identifying and resolving security weaknesses before they can be exploited. I am committed to protecting organizations from emerging cyber threats through proactive monitoring, thorough analysis, and strategic defense implementation.

My curiosity extends beyond surface-level security measures. I am particularly drawn to understanding malware behavior, conducting digital forensics investigations, and architecting secure network infrastructures that can withstand modern attack vectors. Each security challenge represents an opportunity to learn, adapt, and strengthen defensive capabilities.
